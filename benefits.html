Challenges Faced in the Tech World

There are a number of disadvantages to using technology in school environments, most among them being the possibility of distractions during class. These distractions have the capacity to quickly divert our attention from academic activities and direct it towards interactions with social media, gaming platforms, or entertainment content. Furthermore, the accessibility of ChatGPT and related programmes presents an additional difficulty by tempting students to look for quick cuts to finish assignments that call for independent work and critical thinking. The practice of using electronics right before bed can also seriously interfere with our sleep cycles, which will negatively affect our capacity to focus in class the following day.
